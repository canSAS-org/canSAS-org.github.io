The deadline for abstracts is October 12th 2007. Please send your abstracts, which should be no longer than 1 page of US Letter paper (including title, authors, addresses and figures) to cansasv-info@smallangles.net. You should indicate in your email whether you wish to give an talk or a poster.

Below are abstract templates in Word and RTF format[1]. We will also accept abstracts in plain text and LaTeX formats if you include all the information presented in the Word/RTF templates and the document is formatted similarly (margins, font size etc) or the content would fit in the allotted space.
